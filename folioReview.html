Education

My name is Austin Bagley, I'm from Riverton Utah and I'm studying Web Design and Development with an emphasis in Web and App Development at Utah Valley University.

My present education goals include pursuing the completion of my Bachelor's degree from UVU in Web Design and Development. After obtaining my degree I will continue to learn new technology as I enter the industry.

Goals I have to obtain an entry-level position in the industry is to obtain and complete an internship with a development company so I can share and increase my skills. Proceeding the internship I want to obtain an entry-level full-time position in Web Design/Development with a growing company that can add value to my resume and portfolio. Both of these goals have been accomplished.

Long-term career goal for me is to either become the director of a front-end development team or be a senior developer and enjoy doing what I love the most, coding.
